FAQs
What is Lookin’ Up?

Lookin’ Up shares a single, handpicked piece of higher-order inspiration once per day—classic poems, speeches, and more—so you start the morning with something worth thinking about.

How does the “once per day” limit work?

You’ll see one selection per day to encourage focus and reflection. No feeds, no scrolling—just one meaningful thing.

Are my reflections private?

Yes. Reflection space is private. There is no social sharing or public profile.

How do I submit content ideas?

Email suggestions to kaneaidan12@gmail.com. Include a link, title, and why it matters to you.

Is the app free?

Yes—completely free.

Which languages are supported?

English only for now.

Privacy

Lookin’ Up collects no personal data for sharing. If you email support, your message is used solely to respond to your request. For App Store review purposes: the app’s reflection feature is local/private and not shared.
